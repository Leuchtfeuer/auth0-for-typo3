============
Contributing
============

You can contribute by making a **pull request** to the
`master branch <https://github.com/bitmotion/auth0-for-typo3/tree/master>`__ of the repository.
Or just send us some **beers** üçª...

Forms
=====
There is a way to update users metadata using the TYPO3 form framework. A registration and "password forgotten" form is also
available. If you are interested in that, do not hesitate to `contact <https://www.bitmotion.de/en/contact/>`__ us.

Documentation
=============
You are welcome to help improve this guide. Just click on "Edit me on GitHub" on the top right to submit your change request.
